Seven Wonders of world

  • Great Wall of China
  • Christ the Redeemer
  • Colosseum
  • Chichen Itza
  • Machu Picchu
  • Taj Mahal
  • Petra

1. Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials, generally built along an east-to-west line across the historical northern borders of China.

2. Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer is a statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. It is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

3. Colosseum

The Colosseum is an oval amphitheatre in the centre of Rome, Italy. It is the largest ancient amphitheatre ever built.

4. Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is a large archaeological site and UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Yucatan, Mexico.

5. Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 16th-century Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains in Peru.

6. Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the River Yamuna in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.

7. Petra

Petra is a historical and archaeological city in southern Jordan. It is famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system.
